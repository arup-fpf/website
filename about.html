About the Studio

Form follows function.
Function follows form.

Studio Form + Function is an architecture, interior, and planning practice founded in 2021, working across scales — from intimate interiors to large-scale hospitality.

Our Ideology

Two design philosophies have influenced our process: Form Follows Function and Function Follows Form. We contend there is a symbiotic relationship between the two — advocating for the pursuit of both exceptional architectural form and a seamlessly functional design.

Every project begins with the same question: how do space and experience become one? We believe beauty and utility are inseparable, and the best buildings are built to be experienced, not merely observed.
