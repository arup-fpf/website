Retrofit Office, Sonipat

Sonipat, India
LocationSonipat, India
TypeInterior · Workplace
StudioForm + Function
Associated with SPA Design Pvt. Ltd.

A contemporary workplace envisioned as a benchmark for the evolving corporate environment. Designed to foster collaboration, focus, and well-being, the project reimagines the modern office as a seamless interplay of openness, flexibility, and refined spatial experiences.

Rather than relying solely on contemporary aesthetics, the design celebrates a balanced material palette where warm timber finishes, transparent glass enclosures, and exposed structural elements coexist with clean, minimal detailing. Thoughtfully planned workstations, collaborative zones, and private cabins create a fluid workspace that adapts to diverse modes of working while maintaining visual connectivity throughout.

Natural light, uninterrupted sightlines, and integrated greenery soften the architectural language, cultivating an environment that is both productive and inviting. The expressive linear lighting and exposed ceiling reinforce the workspace's contemporary identity, while carefully orchestrated circulation and spatial planning ensure effortless movement across the office.

The result is a workplace that transcends functionality with an environment that embodies innovation, encourages meaningful interaction, and reflects a forward-thinking vision of modern work.
